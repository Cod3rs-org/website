**When:** November 13, 2025, 1:00 PM - 4:00 PM CET  
**Where:** Business Academy Southwest, Spangsbjerg Kirkevej 103, Esbjerg, Denmark

### About the Event

Hack Days in Esbjerg is powered by Google Gemini and hosted by Cod3rs. Whether you're a first-time hacker or a seasoned developer, this event welcomes ALL students in Esbjerg to come together and build something amazing in just 3 hours.

This is your chance to:
- **Learn and experiment** with cutting-edge AI tools
- **Network** with fellow developers and tech enthusiasts
- **Build** innovative solutions to real-world problems
- **Win** awesome prizes and recognition
- **Have fun** while coding with your peers
