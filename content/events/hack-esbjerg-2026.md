Get ready for an epic 24-hour coding adventure! Hack Esbjerg is a tech hackathon that brings together students and developers from all skill levels to collaborate, innovate, and build something amazing.

### Event Details

**When:** March 19-20, 2026 (24 hours)
**Where:** EASV - Business Academy Southwest, Esbjerg, Denmark
**Duration:** Continuous 24-hour hackathon

<!--more-->

{{< section-accent id="about" >}}
### About Hack Esbjerg

Hack Esbjerg is an exciting 24-hour hackathon event organized by COD3RS in partnership with EASV. Whether you're a seasoned developer or just starting your coding journey, this event welcomes participants of all skill levels to:

- **Collaborate** with teammates to solve real challenges
- **Learn** from mentors and industry experts
- **Build** innovative prototypes and solutions
- **Compete** and pitch your ideas to judges
- **Network** with fellow developers and tech enthusiasts
{{< /section-accent >}}

{{< section-dark id="how-it-works" >}}
### How It Works

1. **Form Teams** - Create teams of 2-5 people
2. **Select Challenges** - Choose from available hackathon challenges
3. **Develop Solutions** - Work continuously for 24 hours to build your prototype
4. **Pitch Your Ideas** - Present your solution to judges
5. **Win Prizes** - Compete for recognition and awards

Throughout the event, you'll have access to mentorship from industry experts who can guide you through your development process.
{{< /section-dark >}}

### What to Expect

- **24-hour continuous coding** - From March 19 at 9 AM to March 20 at 9 AM
- **Hands-on challenges** - Real-world problems to solve
- **Expert mentorship** - Industry professionals available to help
- **Team collaboration** - Work with 2-5 teammates
- **Food and refreshments** - Meals and snacks provided throughout the event

### What to Bring

- Your laptop and charger
- Your coding skills and creativity
- Enthusiasm and team spirit
- A willingness to learn and experiment
- Team members (2-5 people per team)
- Your student ID (if applicable)
